Architecture is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings. It involves a combination of creativity, technical knowledge, and cultural understanding to create structures that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. From ancient monuments to modern skyscrapers, architecture reflects the values and aspirations of societies throughout history.

Modern architecture emphasizes sustainability, innovation, and the use of new materials and technologies. Architects today are challenged to design buildings that are energy-efficient, environmentally friendly, and adaptable to changing needs. Iconic structures like the Burj Khalifa and the Sydney Opera House showcase the limitless possibilities of architectural design.
